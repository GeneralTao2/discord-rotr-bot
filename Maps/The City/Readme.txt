Name: The City
Author: schmitty1800
Max Players: 2

The City as you can well imagine takes place in an urban setting with plenty of buildings to garrison.
There is an airport to the NE with some supplies and tech buildings, and downtown to the SW with more 
supplies and tech buildings.  This map is made for skirmish use only since it contains a .ini file. If 
you wish to play this map online remove the .ini file from the folder.
